What Drives Their Decision
When your audience decides between you and a competitor, these factors carry the most weight:
1
Recovery rates & track record
Documented success rates, case studies, and volume of recoveries. CFOs and controllers want proof of performance before placing accounts.
2
Industry certifications & compliance
CLLA certification, IACC membership, SOC 2 compliance, insurance bonding. In a regulated industry, credentials are table stakes.
3
Fee structure & transparency
Contingency rates, flat-fee options, no-recovery-no-fee guarantees. Decision-makers compare fee structures before shortlisting agencies.
4
Technology & reporting
Real-time client portals, automated status updates, and integration with the client's ERP/accounting systems. Modern creditors expect visibility.
5
Geographic & international reach
Ability to recover across state lines and internationally. Multi-location agencies with global networks win enterprise contracts.
6
Reviews & peer reputation
Google reviews, BBB ratings, Clutch profiles, and LinkedIn endorsements. B2B buyers validate agencies through peer networks and online presence.
